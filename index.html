Hobbyist Developer — Privacy Policy

personal projectno adsno trackingLast updated: May 27, 2026

Who I am

I'm an independent hobbyist developer who builds web and mobile applications in my spare time. These are personal projects created for learning and exploration — not backed by a company or organization.

What data I collect

My apps are designed to work with minimal data collection. I do not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, email address, or location.

Most apps store data locally on your device only (e.g., app preferences, paired devices, macros, keyboard layouts). This data never leaves your device. Apps that offer paid subscriptions are the only exception — see the next section.

Subscription apps (e.g., Bluetooth Mouse, Keyboard & Macro)

When you subscribe to a paid feature, two third parties are involved in processing the transaction:

Google Play Billing handles the actual payment. Your purchase data (account, billing info, payment method) stays with Google under their own privacy policy. I never see your payment details.

RevenueCat is used to verify your subscription status and sync entitlements across reinstalls. RevenueCat receives an anonymous device identifier (a randomly generated UUID — not your name, email, or any personal info) plus transaction events forwarded from Google. This is the minimum data needed to confirm "this device has an active subscription." RevenueCat's own privacy policy is at https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy.

Bluetooth device data, keyboard inputs, mouse movements, macro contents, and pairing information never leave your device — these are processed locally by the Bluetooth HID stack and stored only on your device.

Your right to delete subscription data

Quick reset (in-app): Settings → Subscription → "Reset my subscription data". Regenerates a new anonymous UUID, decoupling the device from any prior subscription records. A 24-hour cooldown prevents repeated resets.

Full server-side deletion (GDPR / KVKK Right to be Forgotten): Settings → Subscription → "Delete my subscription data". A single tap performs two coupled actions at the same time: your anonymous device identifier is copied to the clipboard, and the deletion request form opens automatically — you don't need to copy or navigate manually. Submit the form with the device ID (already on your clipboard) plus your Google Play Order ID (found in your "Google Play Order Receipt" Gmail). The historical record will be removed from RevenueCat's servers within 30 days, as required by GDPR Article 17 and KVKK Article 11.

Ads & tracking

I do not run advertisements. I do not use tracking pixels, analytics SDKs, or behavioral tracking tools. Your usage is your own business.

Children's privacy

My apps are not directed at children under 13. I do not knowingly collect data from children.
