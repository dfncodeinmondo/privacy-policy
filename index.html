Who I am

I'm an independent hobbyist developer who builds web and mobile applications in my spare time. These are personal, non-commercial projects created for learning and exploration — not backed by a company or organization.

What data I collect

My apps are designed to work with minimal or no data collection. I do not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, email address, or location unless explicitly required for a specific feature you opt into.

Some apps may store data locally on your device (e.g. app preferences or saved content). This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Third-party services

Some apps may use third-party services such as Google Play Services or Firebase, which have their own privacy policies. These services may collect certain anonymous usage data as per their own terms.

Ads & tracking

I do not run advertisements in my hobby apps. I do not use tracking pixels, analytics SDKs, or behavioral tracking tools. Your usage is your own business.

Children's privacy

My apps are not directed at children under 13. I do not knowingly collect data from children.
